Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Sign up here

Autumn Holiday Club

This autumn school holiday we are organizing the Autumn Holiday Club from Monday September 30th to Friday October 4th, 10.00am - 14.00pm.

Children ages 7 - 15 can join in on daily autumn and nature themed lectures and experiments such as the autumn treasure hunt, making pumpkin slime, the erupting apple volcano and more!

Sign up here

Young Stars Club

Come explore and experiment with our team of experts!

The museum is excited to present the Young Stars Club where children ages 7 - 15 can join in on learning and having fun with the museums own expert team. There will be different topics every meeting. The meetings are held every Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00.

Participation is free, sign up at the reception desk.
