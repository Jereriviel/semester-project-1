Young Stars Club

Come explore and experiment with our team of experts!

The museum is excited to present the Young Stars Club where children ages 7 - 15 can join in on learning and having fun with the museums own expert team. There will be different topics every meeting.

The meetings are held every Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00.

Participation is free, sign up at the reception desk.
